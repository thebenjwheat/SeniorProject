The Grove at Spring Branch Apartments
in Houston, Texas

 Welcome home to The Grove at Spring Branch Apartments! Settle into one of our beautiful one, two, or three bedroom apartments. If you are searching for a relaxing and inviting space, you're in luck! Every floorplan offers spacious living areas with wood-inspired flooring, a fully-equipped kitchen with quartz countertops, modern white and gray finishes, generous storage space, and a full-sized side-by-side washer/dryer in-unit! Select units also include additional storage and/or a private patio or balcony.

  For your convenience, our gated community offers secure access to the building. Choose the floorplan that fits your lifestyle and call our friendly leasing staff to schedule a tour of your new home. We look forward to seeing you at The Grove at Spring Branch Apartments!
