WELCOME HOME

 Welcome to The Grove at Spring Branch Apartments! Settle into one of our beautiful one, two, or three bedroom apartments. If you are searching for a relaxing and inviting space, you're in luck! Every floorplan offers spacious living areas with wood-inspired flooring, a fully-equipped kitchen with quartz countertops, modern white and gray finishes, generous storage space, and a full-sized side-by-side washer/dryer in-unit! Select units also include additional storage and/or a private patio or balcony.

  For your convenience, our gated community offers secure access to the building. Choose the floorplan that fits your lifestyle and call our friendly leasing staff to schedule a tour of your new home. We look forward to seeing you at The Grove at Spring Branch Apartments!

FLOORPLANS

The Grove at Spring Branch Apartments is as convenient as it is comfortable! We are central to several area schools and employers, including Spring Branch Independent School District, Edgewood Elementary School, Spring Woods Middle School, Northbrook High School, SBISD, Yes Prep, KIPP Academy, University of Houston, Houston Community College - Spring Branch Campus, Rice University, Houston Baptist University, Patina Metals, and Oilfield Chemical Company. The Grove at Spring Branch Apartments is also situated near several shopping, dining, and entertainment options, such as downtown Houston, City Center, The Galleria, Memorial City Mall, Houston Museum of Natural Science, Children's Museum of Houston, and Discovery Green. Our convenient proximity to Beltway 8, 610 Loop, Highway 290, Katy Freeway, and I-10 gives you easy access to all that the area has to offer.

Want to find the right floorplan for you? Contact our friendly leasing staff to set up a personal tour today!
