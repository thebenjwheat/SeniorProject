Community Description

Your new home awaits at The Grove at Spring Branch Apartments in Houston, Texas!

At The Grove at Spring Branch Apartments, we provide convenient amenities for you to take advantage of. Come home to a fully-equipped kitchen with quartz countertops, wood-inspired flooring, modern white and gray finishes, and a full-sized side-by-side washer and dryer in-unit, all featured in our one, two, and three bedroom apartments.

With our top-notch location, you can explore nearby shopping, dining, and entertainment options, such as downtown Houston, City Center, The Galleria, Memorial City Mall, Houston Museum of Natural Science, Children's Museum of Houston, and Discovery Green. The Grove at Spring Branch Apartments is also located just minutes from Beltway 8, Highway 290, 610 Loop, Katy Freeway, and I-10, so all of the sights and attractions of the city are your front door.

We are a pet-friendly community. Come home to The Grove at Spring Branch Apartments today!
