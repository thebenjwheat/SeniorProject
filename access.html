Website Accessibility Statement

groveatspringbranchapartments.com

Introduction

Welcome to the groveatspringbranchapartments.com website (the “Website”), which is owned and operated by RentVision, LLC d/b/a RentVision (“RentVision” or “we” or “us”) for the property managed by Grove at Spring Branch, LLC ("Property Management Company"). Our purpose is to consider others as more important than ourselves by providing remarkable experiences for both our clients and their residents. This purpose influences everything we do, and we are committed to ensuring our digital marketing solutions meet relevant accessibility standards and that we continue to improve the user experience for everyone that visits this Website.

Conformance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. We are committed to providing access to our Website for individuals with disabilities, which is why we strive to keep up with industry standards regarding accessibility of the Website and are constantly working to improve. Whether you are using assistive technologies like a screen reader, a magnifier, or voice recognition software, our goal is to make your use of our technology a successful and enjoyable experience.

Exceptions

While we strive to provide an accessible experience on this Website, there may be elements on this Website that are not within our control and ability to make fully accessible. These include content provided by the Property Management Company, third-party links, inline frames, feeds, and embedded content that may be provided within or from this Website to other websites or services (e.g., maps, feeds, videos, widgets, applications, resident portals, etc.). We provide these as a convenience to our users and do not warrant the accessibility of third-party websites and content.

Ongoing Efforts

These efforts are ongoing and our work here is not done. RentVision is continuously working to increase the usability and accessibility of this Website.

Feedback

Questions about the accessibility of the physical location should be sent via the Contact page. If you encounter a problem using or accessing any element of this Website, or would like to provide feedback regarding how we can better accommodate your use of the Website, please contact us at accessibility@rentvision.com. Please indicate the nature of your accessibility problem, the web address of the inaccessible material, and your contact information so that we may respond in a manner most helpful to you.
